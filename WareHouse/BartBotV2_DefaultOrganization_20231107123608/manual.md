# Simpson Tamagotchi Game

A fun and interactive Tamagotchi game based on the Simpsons. Choose your pet from Bart, Lisa, Maggie, Homer or Marge Simpson and take care of them!

## Quick Install

No external dependencies are required for this game. Just make sure you have Python installed on your system.

## ðŸ¤” What is this?

This is a Tamagotchi game where you can choose your pet from the Simpsons family. The game is simple and interactive. You need to feed your pet and play with them to keep them alive. If your pet's hunger or boredom level reaches 50, the game is over.

## ðŸŽ® How to Play

1. Run the `main.py` file to start the game.

2. The game will prompt you to choose your pet. Enter the name of the pet you want to choose (Bart, Lisa, Maggie, Homer or Marge).

3. The game will then enter the main loop where you can interact with your pet. You will be prompted with "What do you want to do? (Feed / Play / Exit)".

4. If you choose "Feed", your pet's hunger level will decrease. If you choose "Play", your pet's boredom level will decrease. You can also choose "Exit" to exit the game.

5. The game will update you on your pet's status after every action. Keep an eye on their hunger and boredom levels!

6. The game ends when your pet's hunger or boredom level reaches 50. Try to keep them alive as long as possible!
